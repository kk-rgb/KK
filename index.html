Don't forget about physical activity! It will boost your creativity

Posted on 25th of January 2020

Many people, myself included have fully loaded calendars, but do not forget to exercise at least 3 times a week for one hour! Not only it will benefit your health in various ways but also exercise will help you to increase your creativity. The latter is especially important in my job, beacuse one of my duties is to think of many diffrent disguise and roles for diffrent exercises in computer programming classes. The difficulty is to find time to exercise. Consider schedualing your trainig in the morning even before start of your normal duties. However remember also about getting at least eight hours of sleep per day!
